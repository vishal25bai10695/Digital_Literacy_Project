# Responsible social media use as a college student #

**DO’s**

1.Do use a professional profile

picture and a clear bio that

mentions your studies.

2. Do share your project updates,

like your HackerRank badges or 

GitHub links.

3. Do double-check facts and

sources before hitting the Share

or Retweet button.

4\. Do use privacy settings to control

who can see your personal family

photos.

5. Do engage politely with experts in 

AI and ML to build your professional

network.

**DON’T**

1.Don’t post offensive, disrespectful,

or aggressive comments on public posts.

2. Don’t share your private location, phone

number, or home address publicly.

3. Don’t post photos or videos of others

without asking for their permission first.

4. Don’t complain about your professors,

college, or classmates in public status updates.

5. Don’t click on suspicious links or free gift
